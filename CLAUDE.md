# Instructions

## Project Overview
This is an ambitious simulation of space-faring life to be used for a video game. It will be capable of varying degrees of background and foreground simulation to give the player the impression of a living solar system where life prevails across planets, moons, orbital habitats, spaceships, etc. The creation of systems and dependencies will be curated by AI via specific skills that will help identify all the necessary parts to make a simulation that can provide interesting player experiences without bespoke scripting. This is a new era of software development and I intend to use AI to crunch the complexities down for me.
